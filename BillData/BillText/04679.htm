                                 AN ACT


 
  To designate the Federal building located at 1200 New Jersey Avenue 
  Southeast in Washington, DC, as the ``Norman Yoshio Mineta Federal 
                              Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building located at 1200 New Jersey Avenue Southeast in 
Washington, DC, shall be known and designated as the ``Norman Yoshio 
Mineta Federal Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Norman Yoshio 
Mineta Federal Building''.
