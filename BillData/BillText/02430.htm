                                 A BILL


 
   To amend the Temporary Reauthorization and Study of the Emergency 
    Scheduling of Fentanyl Analogues Act to extend for one year the 
            temporary order for fentanyl-related substances.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Temporary Reauthorization of the 
Emergency Scheduling of Fentanyl Analogues Act''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF TEMPORARY ORDER FOR FENTANYL-RELATED SUBSTANCES.

    Section 2 of the Temporary Reauthorization and Study of the 
Emergency Scheduling of Fentanyl Analogues Act (Public Law 116-114) is 
amended by striking ``May 6, 2021'' and inserting ``May 6, 2022''.
