                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Safe Drinking Water Act with respect to assistance for 
           disadvantaged communities, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Clean Drinking Water Equity Act''.

SEC. 2. ASSISTANCE FOR DISADVANTAGED COMMUNITIES.

    Section 1452(d)(2)(A) of the Safe Drinking Water Act (42 U.S.C. 
300j-12(d)(2)(A)) is amended by striking ``35 percent'' and inserting 
``40 percent''.
