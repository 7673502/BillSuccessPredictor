                                 A BILL


 
To amend the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization 
 Act for Fiscal Year 2021 to require the Secretary of the Treasury to 
  consult with affected financial institutions and governments of the 
Caribbean region when developing the strategy on financial services de-
                    risking required under that law.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Caribbean Stakeholders Engagement 
Act''.

SEC. 2. STRATEGY ON DE-RISKING.

    Section 6215(c)(4) of the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National 
Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021 (31 U.S.C. 5311 note) is 
amended by inserting ``including affected financial institutions and 
governments of the Caribbean region,'' before ``shall develop''.
