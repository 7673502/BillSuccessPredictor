                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act to ensure that an 
 alien unlawfully present in the United States is not eligible for any 
   service through the Federal Emergency Management Food and Shelter 
                    Program, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Curbing Reckless Ill-conceived 
Spending on Illegal Services Act'' or the ``CRISIS Act''.

SEC. 2. EMERGENCY FOOD AND SHELTER LOCAL BOARDS.

    Section 302(b) of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act (49 
U.S.C. 11332(b)) is amended--
            (1) by redesignating paragraphs (4) and (5) as paragraphs 
        (6) and (7); and
            (2) by inserting after paragraph (3) the following:
            ``(4) certify, at least annually to the National Board and 
        publish such certification locally, and ensure that no 
        recipient service provider provides any direct or indirect 
        service, including transportation, to an alien unlawfully 
        present in the United States.''.
