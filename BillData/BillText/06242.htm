                                 A BILL


 
     To amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to clarify that 
    limitations on the scope of judicial review of certain removal 
proceedings apply only in the case of discretionary determinations, and 
                          for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Family Unity Act of 2021''.

SEC. 2. NARROWING OF LIMITATIONS ON THE SCOPE OF JUDICIAL REVIEW OF 
              CERTAIN REMOVAL ACTIONS.

    Section 242(g) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 
1252(g)) is amended by striking ``or claim by or on behalf of any alien 
arising from the decision or action'' and inserting ``or claim by or on 
behalf of any alien challenging the discretionary decision or action''.
