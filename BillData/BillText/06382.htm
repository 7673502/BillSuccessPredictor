                                 A BILL


 
To nullify the order of the Mayor of the District of Columbia imposing 
a vaccine entry requirement for certain establishments and facilities, 
                        and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NULLIFICATION OF VACCINE ENTRY REQUIREMENT OF DISTRICT OF 
              COLUMBIA.

    Order 2021-148 of the Mayor of the District of Columbia (December 
22, 2021) and any successor or substantially similar order of the Mayor 
of the District of Columbia shall have no force or effect.
