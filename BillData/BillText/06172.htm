                                 A BILL


 
   To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, 
               unique ZIP Code for Kinnelon, New Jersey.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SINGLE, UNIQUE ZIP CODE FOR KINNELON, NEW JERSEY.

    Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this 
section, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, 
unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely the 
municipal boundaries of the Borough of Kinnelon, New Jersey.
