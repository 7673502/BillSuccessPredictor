                                 A BILL


 
   To prevent unnecessary delay in firearms transfers from licensed 
                firearms dealers to unlicensed persons.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Ensuring Second Amendment Freedoms 
are Executed Timely Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON PROLONGED DELAY OF TRANSFER CAUSED BY THE 
              GOVERNMENT.

    Section 922(t)(1)(B) of title 18, United States Code is amended by 
striking clause (ii) and inserting the following:
            ``(ii) 3 calendar days have elapsed since the licensee 
        contacted the system, and the system has not notified the 
        licensee that the receipt of a firearm by such other person 
        would violate subsection (g) or (n) of this section; and''.
