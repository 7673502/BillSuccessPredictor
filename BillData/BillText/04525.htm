                                 A BILL


 
To amend title IV-A of the Social Security Act, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Supporting Work Through 
Apprenticeships Act''.

SEC. 2. SUPPORTING WORK THROUGH APPRENTICESHIPS.

    Section 407(d)(5) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 607(d)(5)) 
is amended by inserting ``, including apprenticeship'' before the 
semicolon.

SEC. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE.

    The amendment made by this Act shall take effect on October 1, 
2022.
