                                 A BILL


 
To rescind and transfer funds available for the Office of the Director 
 of the National Institutes of Health to accounts for pediatric cancer 
 research, awareness, and survivorship, to reduce the deficit, and for 
                            other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Fighting Cancer in Children Act''.

SEC. 2. TRANSFER AND RESCISSION OF AMOUNTS AVAILABLE FOR OFFICE OF THE 
              DIRECTOR OF NIH.

    Of the unobligated amounts available for ``National Institutes of 
Health--Office of the Director'' in Public Law 117-103--
            (1) $30,000,000 is transferred to, and merged with, amounts 
        available to carry out section 417E of the Public Health 
        Service Act (42 U.S.C. 285a-11; relating to pediatric cancer 
        research, awareness, and survivorship); and
            (2) $30,000,000 is hereby rescinded.
