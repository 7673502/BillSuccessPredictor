                                 A BILL


 
    To prohibit the Secretary of Homeland Security from rescinding, 
 revising, or suspending the final rule, entitled ``Inadmissibility on 
            Public Charge Grounds'', and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Preserving Safety Net Integrity Act 
of 2021''.

SEC. 2. REQUIRING IMPLEMENTATION OF FINAL RULE.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary of 
Homeland Security may not take any action to rescind, revise, or 
suspend the final rule, entitled ``Inadmissibility on Public Charge 
Grounds'' (85 Fed. Reg. 41292, August 14, 2019).
