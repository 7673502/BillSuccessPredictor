                                 A BILL


 
 To provide it is the policy of the United States that the NATO-Russia 
  Founding Act, signed May 27, 1997, in Paris, does not constrain the 
         deployment of United States or NATO forces in any way.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``United States NATO Force Deployment 
Policy Act of 2022''.

SEC. 2. STATEMENT OF POLICY.

    It is the policy of the United States that the NATO-Russia Founding 
Act, signed May 27, 1997, in Paris, does not constrain the deployment 
of United States or NATO forces in any way.
