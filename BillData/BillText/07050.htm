                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 38, United States Code, to make certain improvements in 
the laws relating to the administration of certain benefits payable to 
        survivors of deceased veterans, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Ernest Peltz Accrued Veterans 
Benefits Act''.

SEC. 2. PAYMENT OF CERTAIN BENEFITS TO SURVIVORS OF DECEASED VETERANS 
              BY SECRETARY OF VETERANS AFFAIRS.

    (a) Payment of Benefits for Month of Death.--Section 5310 of title 
38, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in subsection (a)(1), by striking ``A surviving 
        spouse'' and inserting ``An eligible survivor'';
            (2) in subsection (b), by striking ``surviving spouse'' 
        both places it appears and inserting ``eligible survivor''; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following new subsection:
    ``(c) Eligible Survivor Defined.--In this section, the term 
`eligible survivor' means the living person first listed below:
            ``(1) The veteran's spouse.
            ``(2) The veteran's children (in equal shares).
            ``(3) The veteran's dependent parents (in equal shares).''.
    (b) Accrued Benefits.--Section 5121 of title 38, United States Code 
is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
    ``(d) The amount of the accrued benefit payable under subsection 
(a) on behalf on an individual is the total amount of the periodic 
monetary benefits (other than insurance and servicemen's indemnity) 
under laws administered by the Secretary to which the individual was 
entitled at death for the entire period of time for which the 
individual was entitled to, but did not receive, such benefits.''.
    (c) Effective Date.--The amendments made by this section shall take 
effect on the date of the enactment of this Act and shall apply with 
respect to deaths that occur on or after that date.
