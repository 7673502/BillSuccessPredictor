                                 A BILL


 
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to increase funding for 
 quality measure endorsement, input, and selection under the Medicare 
                    program, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Promoting Health Care Quality Act of 
2022''.

SEC. 2. FUNDING QUALITY MEASUREMENT PROGRAMS.

    Section 1890 of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395aaa) is 
amended in subparagraph (d)(2), by striking ``$20,000,000 for fiscal 
year 2023'' and inserting ``$26,000,000 for fiscal year 2023''.
