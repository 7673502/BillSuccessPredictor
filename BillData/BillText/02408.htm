                                 A BILL


 
To extend the authorization for Arabia Mountain National Heritage Area.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION EXTENDED FOR ARABIA MOUNTAIN NATIONAL HERITAGE 
              AREA.

    Section 240 the National Heritage Areas Act of 2006 (Public Law 
109-338; 16 U.S.C. 461 note) is amended by striking ``terminates on'' 
and all that follows through the final period and inserting ``October 
12, 2036''.
