                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit any entity from imposing a mandate that individuals be 
          vaccinated against COVID-19, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``No COVID-19 Vaccination Mandate Act 
of 2021''.

SEC. 2. NO MANDATES FOR VACCINATION AGAINST COVID-19.

    Notwithstanding any Federal or State law, no institution or other 
entity, including any such Federal, State, local, Tribal, or private 
entity, may establish, implement, or enforce any mandate (including as 
a condition of employment, enrollment or attendance at school, or 
receipt of services) that any individual be vaccinated against SARS-
Cov-2 or COVID-19.
