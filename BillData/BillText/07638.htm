                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  6000 South Florida Avenue in Lakeland, Florida, as the ``U.S. Marine 
               Corporal Ronald R. Payne Jr. Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. U.S. MARINE CORPORAL RONALD R. PAYNE JR. POST OFFICE.
    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 6000 South Florida Avenue in Lakeland, Florida, shall be 
known and designated as the ``U.S. Marine Corporal Ronald R. Payne Jr. 
Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``U.S. 
Marine Corporal Ronald R. Payne Jr. Post Office''.
