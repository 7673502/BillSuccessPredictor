                                 A BILL


 
     To amend the Communities Helping Invest through Property and 
   Improvements Needed for Veterans Act of 2016 to extend the pilot 
                    program authorized by such Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Communities Helping Invest through 
Property and Improvements Needed for Veterans Act'' or the ``CHIP IN 
for Veterans Act''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF PILOT PROGRAM RELATING TO REAL PROPERTY AND 
              FACILITIES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS.

    Subsection (i) of section 2 of the Communities Helping Invest 
through Property and Improvements Needed for Veterans Act of 2016 
(Public Law 114-294) is amended by striking ``5 years'' and inserting 
``10 years''.
