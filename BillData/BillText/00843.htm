                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Commodity Credit Corporation Charter Act to raise the 
          borrowing limit of the Commodity Credit Corporation.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. RAISING THE BORROWING LIMIT OF THE COMMODITY CREDIT 
              CORPORATION.

    Section 4(i) of the Commodity Credit Corporation Charter Act (15 
U.S.C. 714b(i)) is amended by striking ``$30,000,000,000'' and 
inserting ``$68,000,000,000''.
