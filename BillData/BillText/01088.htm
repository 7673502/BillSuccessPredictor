                                 A BILL


 
To direct the Secretary of Defense to implement certain recommendations 
regarding screening individuals who seek to enlist in the Armed Forces.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Shielding our Military from 
Extremists Act''.

SEC. 2. IMPLEMENTATION OF CERTAIN RECOMMENDATIONS REGARDING SCREENING 
              INDIVIDUALS WHO SEEK TO ENLIST IN THE ARMED FORCES.

    Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this 
Act, the Secretary of Defense shall implement the seven recommendations 
of the Under Secretary of Defense for Personnel and Readiness on page 2 
of the report titled ``Screening Individuals Who Seek to Enlist in the 
Armed Forces'', submitted to the Committees on Armed Services of the 
Senate and House of Representatives on October 14, 2020.
