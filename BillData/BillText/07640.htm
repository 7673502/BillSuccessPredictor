                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Orphan Drug Act to reauthorize a program of grants and 
contracts for the development of drugs for rare diseases and conditions 
              (commonly referred to as ``orphan drugs'').

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REAUTHORIZATION OF GRANTS AND CONTRACTS FOR DEVELOPMENT OF 
              ORPHAN DRUGS.

    Section 5(c) of the Orphan Drug Act (21 U.S.C. 360ee(c)) is amended 
by striking ``2018 through 2022'' and inserting ``2023 through 2027''.
