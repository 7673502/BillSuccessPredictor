                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Hudson River Valley National Heritage Area Act of 1996 
(Public Law 104-333; 54 U.S.C. 320101 note) to include all of Saratoga 
 and Washington Counties in the boundaries of the Hudson River Valley 
                        National Heritage Area.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Maurice D. Hinchey Hudson River 
Valley National Heritage Area Enhancement Act''.

SEC. 2. HUDSON RIVER VALLEY NATIONAL HERITAGE AREA.

    Section 904(b) of the Hudson River Valley National Heritage Area 
Act of 1996 (Public Law 104-333; 54 U.S.C. 320101 note) is amended to 
read as follows:
    ``(b) Boundaries.--The Heritage Area shall be comprised of the 
counties of Albany, Rensselaer, Columbia, Greene, Ulster, Dutchess, 
Orange, Putnam, Westchester, Rockland, Saratoga, and Washington, New 
York.''.
