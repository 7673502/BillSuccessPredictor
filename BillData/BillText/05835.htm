                                 A BILL


 
   To prohibit the use of Federal funds to establish, implement, or 
                      enforce any vaccine mandate.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Defund Federal Vaccine Mandates 
Act''.

SEC. 2. NO FEDERAL FUNDS FOR ANY VACCINE MANDATE.

    No Federal funds may be used to establish, implement, or enforce 
any vaccine mandate.
