                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 40, United States Code, to include Wyoming County in the 
  region covered by the Northern Border Regional Commission, and for 
                            other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NORTHERN BORDER REGIONAL COMMISSION EXPANSION.

    Section 15733(3) of title 40, United States Code, is amended by 
inserting ``Wyoming,'' after ``Wayne,''.
