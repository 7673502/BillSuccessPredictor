                                 A BILL


 
    To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend authority for 
 supportive services for very low-income veteran families in permanent 
                                housing.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Preventing Homelessness for Veteran 
Families Act''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF AUTHORITY FOR SUPPORTIVE SERVICES FOR VERY LOW-
              INCOME VETERAN FAMILIES IN PERMANENT HOUSING.

    Section 2044(e)(1) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
adding at the end the following new subparagraphs:
            ``(I) $731,000,000 for fiscal year 2023.
            ``(J) $775,000,000 for fiscal year 2024.''.
