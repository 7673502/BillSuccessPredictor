                                 A BILL


 
     To extend the temporary order for fentanyl-related substances.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF TEMPORARY ORDER FOR FENTANYL-RELATED 
              SUBSTANCES.

    Effective as if included in the enactment of the Temporary 
Reauthorization and Study of the Emergency Scheduling of Fentanyl 
Analogues Act (Public Law 116-114), section 2 of such Act (as amended 
by Public Law 117-103) is amended by striking ``December 31, 2022'' and 
inserting ``December 31, 2024''.
