                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit the obligation or expenditure of funds relating to the 
Climate Change Professionals Program and any other program, project, or 
   activity relating to climate change of the Department of Homeland 
                   Security, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION RELATING TO CLIMATE CHANGE PROFESSIONALS PROGRAM 
              AND ANY OTHER PROGRAM, PROJECT, OR ACTIVITY RELATING TO 
              CLIMATE CHANGE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF HOMELAND SECURITY.

    The Secretary of Homeland Security may not obligate or expend any 
funds--
            (1) to carry out the Climate Change Professionals Program 
        of the Department of Homeland Security;
            (2) to establish or maintain a program to train individuals 
        relating to climate change; or
            (3) for any program, project, or activity relating to 
        climate change.
