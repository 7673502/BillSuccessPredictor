                                 A BILL


 
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a new ZIP Code 
                 for certain communities in Wisconsin.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATING NEW ZIP CODE.

    Not later than 270 days after the enactment of this Act, the United 
States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code for, as 
nearly as practicable, each of the following communities:
            (1) Village of Somers, Wisconsin.
            (2) Village of Mount Pleasant, Wisconsin.
            (3) Village of Caledonia, Wisconsin.
