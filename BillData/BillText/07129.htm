                                 A BILL


 
          To prohibit the importation of crude oil from Iran.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON IMPORTATION OF CRUDE OIL FROM IRAN.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, all crude oil from Iran 
shall be banned from importation into the United States.
