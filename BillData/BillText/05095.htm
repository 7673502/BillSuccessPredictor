                                 A BILL


 
 To amend title 10, United States Code, to improve how members of the 
 reserve components of the Armed Forces performing active duty or full-
 time National Guard duty are counted towards authorized end strengths.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ACCOUNTING OF RESERVE COMPONENT MEMBERS PERFORMING ACTIVE 
              DUTY OR FULL-TIME NATIONAL GUARD DUTY TOWARDS AUTHORIZED 
              END STRENGTHS.

    Section 115(b)(2)(B) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``1095 days in the previous 1460 days'' and inserting ``1825 
days in the previous 2190 days''.
