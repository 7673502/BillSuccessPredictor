                                 AN ACT


 
To amend title 38, United States Code, to expand eligibility of members 
of the National Guard for housing loans guaranteed by the Secretary of 
                           Veterans Affairs.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Expanding Home Loans for Guard and 
Reservists Act''.

SEC. 2. EXPANSION OF ELIGIBILITY OF MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL GUARD FOR 
              HOUSING LOANS GUARANTEED BY THE SECRETARY OF VETERANS 
              AFFAIRS.

    Section 3701(b)(7) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``full-time National Guard duty'' and inserting ``active 
service''.

SEC. 3. DETERMINATION OF BUDGETARY EFFECTS.

    The budgetary effects of this Act, for the purpose of complying 
with the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010, shall be determined by 
reference to the latest statement titled ``Budgetary Effects of PAYGO 
Legislation'' for this Act, submitted for printing in the Congressional 
Record by the Chairman of the House Budget Committee, provided that 
such statement has been submitted prior to the vote on passage.
