                                 A BILL


 
To make technical changes clarifying section 503 of the Federal Credit 
                          Reform Act of 1990.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TECHNICAL CHANGES WITH RESPECT TO COST ESTIMATES.

    Section 503(d) of the Federal Credit Reform Act of 1990 (2 U.S.C. 
661b(d)) is amended by striking the period at the end and inserting ``, 
which shall include the percent guaranteed on loan guarantee 
commitments.''.
