                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
 at 1535 East Los Ebanos Boulevard in Brownsville, Texas, as the ``1st 
           Lieutenant Andres Zermeno Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. 1ST LIEUTENANT ANDRES ZERMENO POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 1535 East Los Ebanos Boulevard in Brownsville, Texas, shall 
be known and designated as the ``1st Lieutenant Andres Zermeno Post 
Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``1st 
Lieutenant Andres Zermeno Post Office Building''.
