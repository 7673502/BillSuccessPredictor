                                 A BILL


 
   To codify the Industry-Recognized Apprenticeship Programs of the 
                          Department of Labor.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Modernizing America's Apprenticeship 
Act''.

SEC. 2. INDUSTRY-RECOGNIZED APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAMS OF THE DEPARTMENT 
              OF LABOR.

    The provisions of part 29 of title 29, Code of Federal Regulations, 
as amended by the final regulations entitled ``Apprenticeship Programs, 
Labor Standards for Registration, Amendment of Regulations'' published 
by the Department of Labor in the Federal Register on March 11, 2020 
(85 Fed. Reg. 14294 et seq.), and as in effect on the day before the 
date of enactment of this Act, shall have the force and effect of 
enacted law.
