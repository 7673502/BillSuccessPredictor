                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit the use of Federal funds to establish a Disinformation 
                           Governance Board.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON ESTABLISHMENT OF A DISINFORMATION GOVERNANCE 
              BOARD.

    No Federal funds authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made 
available to the Secretary of Homeland Security may be made available 
for the establishment of a Disinformation Governance Board.
