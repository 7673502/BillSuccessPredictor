                                 An Act


 
    To amend the Fentanyl Sanctions Act, to modify certain deadlines 
  relating to the Commission on Combating Synthetic Opioid Trafficking.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. MODIFICATION OF CERTAIN DEADLINES FOR COMMISSION ON 
COMBATING SYNTHETIC OPIOID TRAFFICKING.
    Section 7221(f)(2) of the Fentanyl Sanctions Act (133 Stat. 2273) 
is amended by striking ``270 days'' and inserting ``390 days''.
