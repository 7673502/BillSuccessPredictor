                                 An Act


 
 To designate the Federal Building and United States Courthouse located 
 at 1125 Chapline Street in Wheeling, West Virginia, as the ``Frederick 
     P. Stamp, Jr. Federal Building and United States Courthouse''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.
    The Federal building and United States courthouse located at 1125 
Chapline Street in Wheeling, West Virginia, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Frederick P. Stamp, Jr. Federal building and United 
States Courthouse''.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.
    Any reference in law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference 
to the ``Frederick P. Stamp, Jr. Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse''.
