                                 A BILL


 
 To direct the United States Postal Service to designate 77416 as the 
  single, unique ZIP Code for Sargent, Texas, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ZIP CODE FOR SARGENT, TEXAS.

    Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, 
the United States Postal Service shall designate 77416 as the single, 
unique ZIP Code applicable to Sargent, Texas.
