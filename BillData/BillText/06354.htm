                                 A BILL


 
   To direct the Secretary of State to submit a report on the United 
         Nations Human Rights Council, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Fix the United Nations Human Rights 
Council Act''.

SEC. 2. REPORT ON UNITED NATIONS HUMAN RIGHTS COUNCIL.

    (a) In General.--Not later than 90 days after the date of the 
enactment of this Act, the Secretary of State shall submit to the 
Committee on Foreign Affairs of the House of Representatives and the 
Committee on Foreign Relations of the Senate a report on United States 
policy towards China, Russia, Cuba, Venezuela, and other human rights 
violators relating to their roles as members of the United Nations 
Human Rights Council. Such report shall include--
            (1) a section relating to the United States plan to 
        eliminate Item 7 of the United Nations Human Rights Council 
        Agenda;
            (2) a section relating to the United States areas of 
        concern related to China, Russia, Cuba, Venezuela, and other 
        human rights violators who are members of the United Nations 
        Human Rights Council; and
            (3) a section relating to the United States plan to remove 
        China, Russia, Cuba, Venezuela, and other human rights 
        violators from the United Nations Human Rights Council.
    (b) Form.--The report required under subsection (a) shall be 
submitted in unclassified form but may include a classified annex.
