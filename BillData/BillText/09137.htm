                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize the State offices 
                        of rural health program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``State Offices of Rural Health 
Program Reauthorization Act of 2022''.

SEC. 2. STATE OFFICES OF RURAL HEALTH.

    Section 338J(i)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 
254r(i)(1)) is amended by striking ``$12,500,000 for each of fiscal 
years 2018 through 2022'' and inserting ``$15,000,000 for each of 
fiscal years 2023 through 2027''.
