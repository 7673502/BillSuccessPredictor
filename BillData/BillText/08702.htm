                                 A BILL


 
     To prohibit the implementation of the proposed rule entitled 
        ``Nondiscrimination in Health Programs and Activities''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Taxpayers Support Life Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITING IMPLEMENTATION OF PROPOSED RULE ENTITLED 
              ``NONDISCRIMINATION IN HEALTH PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES''.

    (a) In General.--The Secretary of Health and Human Services may not 
finalize, implement, administer, or enforce the proposed rule entitled 
``Nondiscrimination in Health Programs and Activities'' (RIN: 0945-
AA17).
    (b) Limitations on Funding.--No Federal funds may be used--
            (1) to finalize, implement, administer, or enforce the 
        proposed rule described in subsection (a); or
            (2) in contravention of administering or enforcing the 
        final rule entitled ``Nondiscrimination in Health and Health 
        Education Programs or Activities, Delegation of Authority'' (85 
        Fed. Reg. 37160) and dated June 19, 2020.
