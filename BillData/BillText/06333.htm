                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit the use of Federal funds to establish, maintain, or support 
any national database identifying individuals as having received or not 
                         received any vaccine.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NO FEDERAL FUNDS FOR NATIONAL DATABASE IDENTIFYING 
              VACCINATED OR UNVACCINATED INDIVIDUALS.

    No Federal funds may be used to establish, maintain, or support any 
national database identifying individuals as having received or not 
received any vaccine.
