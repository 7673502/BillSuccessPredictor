                                 A BILL


 
           To prohibit funding for the World Economic Forum.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Defund Davos Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON FUNDING.

    No funds available to the Department of State, the United States 
Agency for International Development, or any other department or agency 
may be used to provide funding for the World Economic Forum.
