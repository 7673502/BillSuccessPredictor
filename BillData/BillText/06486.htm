                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Ethics in Government Act of 1978 to modify the timing to 
       file periodic transaction reports, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Transparency in Government Officials 
Trading Act''.

SEC. 2. MODIFICATION OF TIMING FOR PERIODIC TRANSACTION REPORTS.

    (a) Timing.--Section 103(l) of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978 
(5 U.S.C. App. 103(l)) is amended--
            (1) by striking ``30 days after receiving notification of 
        any transaction'' and inserting ``24 hours after conducting any 
        transaction'';
            (2) by striking ``, but in no case later than 45 days after 
        such transaction''; and
            (3) by inserting ``electronically'' after ``transaction''.
    (b) Penalty.--Section 104(d) of the Ethics in Government Act of 
1978 (5 U.S.C. App. 104(d)) is amended--
            (1) by redesignating paragraph (2) as paragraph (3); and
            (2) by inserting after paragraph (1) the following:
    ``(2) Any individual who, as determined by the supervising ethics 
office, fails to file a report under section 103(l) within 24 hours of 
the date such report is required to be filed, shall pay a fee equal to 
the amount of the transaction for which such report was required to be 
filed.''.
