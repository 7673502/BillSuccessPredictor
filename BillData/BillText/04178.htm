                                 A BILL


 
 To extend the authority to collect Shasta-Trinity Marina fees through 
                           fiscal year 2027.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHASTA-TRINITY MARINA FEES.

    Section 422 of the Department of the Interior, Environment, and 
Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2008 (Public Law 110-161; 121 Stat 
2149) as amended, is further amended by striking ``and each subsequent 
fiscal year through fiscal year 2019'' and inserting ``and each 
subsequent fiscal year through fiscal year 2027''.
