                                 A BILL


 
 To codify a final rule of the United States Fish and Wildlife Service 
 relating to endangered and threatened species, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Threatened Species Protection 
Improvement Act of 2021''.

SEC. 2. CODIFICATION OF FINAL RULE.

    The revisions to the Code of Federal Regulations made pursuant to 
the final rule of the United States Fish and Wildlife Service of the 
Department of the Interior, titled ``Endangered and Threatened Wildlife 
and Plants; Regulations for Prohibitions to Threatened Wildlife and 
Plants'' and published in the Federal Register on August 27, 2019 (84 
Fed. Reg. 44753), shall have the same force and effect of law as if 
enacted by an Act of Congress.
