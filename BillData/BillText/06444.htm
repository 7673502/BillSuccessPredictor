                                 A BILL


 
  To provide for the conveyance of a small parcel of real property at 
     Beale Air Force Base in California to the Three Rivers Levee 
                         Improvement Authority.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LAND CONVEYANCE, BEALE AIR FORCE BASE, CALIFORNIA.

    (a) Conveyance Required.--Not later than 60 days after the date on 
which the Three Rivers Levee Improvement Authority submits a written 
request to the Secretary of the Air Force to proceed with the 
conveyance described in this section, the Secretary shall convey to the 
Three Rivers Levee Improvement Authority all right, title, and interest 
of the United States in and to the parcel of real property consisting 
of approximately 0.218 acres at Beale Air Force Base in California and 
identified as ``Impacted Area'' on the map entitled ``T.R.L.I.A. 200-
year Goldfields Levee Project, Proposed Easement Acquisition from Beale 
A.F.B.'' and dated May 1, 2020, for the purpose of permitting the Three 
Rivers Levee Improvement Authority to use the property for the flood 
damage reduction project, Yuba River Basin, California, authorized by 
section 101(a)(10) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1999 
(Public Law 106-53; 113 Stat. 275), as modified by section 1305 of the 
America's Water Infrastructure Act of 2018 (Public Law 115-270).
    (b) Form of Conveyance and Consideration.--The Secretary of the Air 
Force shall convey the real property described in subsection (a) by 
quitclaim deed and without consideration.
    (c) Map.--
            (1) Corrections.--The Secretary of the Air Force may make 
        clerical and typographical corrections to the map referred to 
        in subsection (a). Such map and any revised map prepared under 
        the authority of this paragraph shall have the same force and 
        effect as if included as part of this Act.
            (2) Public availability.--The map referred to in subsection 
        (a) and any revised map prepared under the authority of 
        paragraph (1) shall be available for public inspection on a 
        website of the Department of the Air Force.
    (d) Survey.--The exact acreage and legal description of the real 
property to be conveyed under subsection (a) shall be determined by a 
survey satisfactory to the Secretary of the Air Force. The cost of the 
survey shall be borne by the Three Rivers Levee Improvement Authority.
    (e) Additional Terms And Conditions.--The conveyance under 
subsection (a) shall be subject to any other terms and conditions that 
the Secretary of the Air Force considers appropriate to protect the 
interests of the United States.
