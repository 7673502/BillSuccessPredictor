                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Federal Meat Inspection Act to allow the interstate sale 
            of State-inspected meat, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Expanding Markets for State-
Inspected Meat Processors Act of 2021''.

SEC. 2. STATE-INSPECTED MEAT.
