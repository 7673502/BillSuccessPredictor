                                 A BILL


 
To require that all Special Drawing Rights allocations be authorized by 
                            Congress in law.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Special Drawing Rights Oversight 
Act''.

SEC. 2. CONGRESSIONAL APPROVAL REQUIRED FOR ALL ALLOCATIONS OF SPECIAL 
              DRAWING RIGHTS.

    Section 6(a) of the Special Drawing Rights Act (22 U.S.C. 286q(a)) 
is amended by striking ``so that allocations'' and all that follows and 
inserting a period.
