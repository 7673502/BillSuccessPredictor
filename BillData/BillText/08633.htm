                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Water Resources Development Act of 1992 with respect to 
  stormwater management for the city of East Point, Georgia, and for 
                            other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``City of East Point Stormwater 
Management Act''.

SEC. 2. EAST POINT, GEORGIA, STORMWATER MANAGEMENT.

    Section 219(f)(136) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1992 
(106 Stat. 4835; 113 Stat. 336; 121 Stat. 1261) is amended by striking 
``$5,000,000 for'' and inserting ``$15,000,000 for stormwater 
management and other''.
