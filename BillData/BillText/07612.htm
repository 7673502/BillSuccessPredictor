                                 A BILL


 
To advance desalination research and technological innovation, and for 
                            other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Desalination Research Advancement 
Act''.

SEC. 2. DESALINATION RESEARCH AUTHORIZATION.

    (a) Section 8 of the Water Desalination Act of 1996 (42 U.S.C. 
10301 note; Public Law 104-298) is amended in subsection (a)--
            (1) by striking ``$5,000,000 per year for fiscal years 1997 
        through 2021'' and inserting ``$20,000,000 per year for fiscal 
        years 2022 through 2026''; and
            (2) by striking ``$1,000,000'' and inserting 
        ``$15,000,000''.
    (b) Section 3(e) of the Water Desalination Act of 1996 is amended--
            (1) in paragraph (5), by striking ``and'';
            (2) in paragraph (6), by striking the period at the end and 
        inserting ``; and''; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
            ``(7) to minimize the impacts of seawater desalination on 
        aquatic life and coastal ecosystems, including technologies to 
        monitor and reduce those impacts.''.
