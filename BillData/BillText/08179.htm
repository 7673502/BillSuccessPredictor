                                 A BILL


 
To amend section 242 of the National Housing Act to provide parity with 
 respect to access to the mortgage insurance for hospitals program for 
              licensed hospitals, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Securing Facilities for Mental 
Health Services Act''.

SEC. 2. MORTGAGE INSURANCE FOR HOSPITALS PARITY.

    (a) In General.--Section 242(b)(1) of the National Housing Act (12 
U.S.C. 1715z-7(b)(1)) is amended--
            (1) in subparagraph (A), by striking the semicolon at the 
        end and inserting ``; and'';
            (2) by striking subparagraph (B); and
            (3) by redesignating subparagraph (C) as subparagraph (B).
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendments made by this section shall 
apply upon the date of the enactment of this Act.
