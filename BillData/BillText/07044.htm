                                 A BILL


 
     To prohibit importation of nickel and copper from the Russian 
                              Federation.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Not a Red Cent from Russia Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON IMPORTATION OF NICKEL AND COPPER FROM THE 
              RUSSIAN FEDERATION.

    Effective on the date of the enactment of this Act, the President 
shall prohibit importation into the United States of nickel and copper 
from the Russian Federation.
