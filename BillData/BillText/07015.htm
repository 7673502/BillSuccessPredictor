                                 A BILL


 
 To provide for the conveyance of seized Russian and Belarusian assets 
                       to the people of Ukraine.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Confiscating Corrupt Criminal 
Proceeds (CCCP) Act of 2022''.

SEC. 2. USE OF CERTAIN FORFEITED ASSETS.

    Section 981(e) of title 18, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in paragraph (6), by striking ``or'' at the end;
            (2) in paragraph (7), by striking the period at the end and 
        inserting ``; or''; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following:
            ``(8) in the case of any property owned by a Russian or 
        Belarusian national, to the Administrator of the United States 
        Agency for International Development, to be made available to 
        provide humanitarian assistance to the people of Ukraine.''.
