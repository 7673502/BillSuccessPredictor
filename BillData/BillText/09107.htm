                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 46, United States Code, to require a State to enter into 
 a data sharing agreement with the Secretary of Homeland Security and 
 the Secretary of Transportation in order to be eligible for the port 
security grant program and the port infrastructure development program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``States Helping Apprehend Rogue 
Exports Act''.

SEC. 2. STATE DEPARTMENT OF MOTOR VEHICLE DATA SHARING AGREEMENTS.

    (a) Port Infrastructure Development Program.--Section 54301(a)(5) 
of title 46, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the 
following:
                    ``(C) State department of motor vehicle data 
                sharing agreements.--To be eligible for a grant under 
                this subsection or subsection (b), the State or States 
                with respect to which a grant is being provided shall 
                have in effect a data sharing agreement that requires 
                the State or States to provide, upon request of the 
                Secretary of Homeland Security or the Secretary of 
                Transportation, identifying information regarding any 
                motor vehicle registered in the State, including 
                registration, title, and vehicle identification 
                number.''.
    (b) Port Security Grant Program.--Section 70107(i) of title 46, 
United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
            ``(5) State department of motor vehicle data sharing 
        agreements.--To be eligible for a grant under this section, the 
        State in which the entity subject to an Area Maritime 
        Transportation Security Plan is located shall have in effect a 
        data sharing agreement that requires the State to provide, upon 
        request of the Secretary of Homeland Security or the Secretary 
        of Transportation, identifying information regarding any motor 
        vehicle registered in the State, including registration, title, 
        and vehicle identification number.''.
    (c) Airport Improvement Program.--Section 47106(a) of title 49, 
United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in paragraph (6)(E) by striking ``and'' at the end;
            (2) in paragraph (7)(D) by striking the period at the end 
        and inserting ``; and''; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following:
            ``(8) the State in which the project is located has in 
        effect a data sharing agreement with the Secretary of Homeland 
        Security and the Secretary of Transportation that requires the 
        State to provide, upon request of either such Secretary, 
        identifying information regarding any motor vehicle registered 
        in the State, including registration, title, and vehicle 
        identification number.''.
