                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Uniform Time Act to make daylight savings time permanent.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``SPF Act''.

SEC. 2. MAKE DAYLIGHT SAVINGS TIME PERMANENT.

    Section 3(a) of the Uniform Time Act of 1966 (15 U.S.C. 260a(a)) is 
amended by striking ``During the period commencing at 2 o'clock 
antemeridian on the second Sunday of March of each year and ending at 2 
o'clock antemeridian on the first Sunday of November of each year'' and 
inserting ``Starting the first Sunday in November 2021 permanently 
advancing one hour and such time as so advanced shall for the purposes 
of such sections 261 to 264, as so modified, be the standard time of 
such zone during such period''.
