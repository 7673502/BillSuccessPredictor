                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Claims Resolution Act to make a technical correction to 
   the Taos Pueblo Water Development Fund and the Aamodt Settlement 
                 Pueblos' Fund, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION OF PAYMENT OF ADJUSTED INTEREST ON THE TAOS 
              PUEBLO WATER DEVELOPMENT FUND.

    Section 509(c)(1) of the Claims Resolution Act (Public Law 111-291) 
is amended by adding at the end the following:
                    ``(C) Adjusted interest payments.--In addition to 
                the amounts made available under subparagraphs (A) and 
                (B), there is authorized to be appropriated to the 
                Secretary for deposit into the Taos Pueblo Water 
                Development Fund $7,794,297.52.''.

SEC. 2. AUTHORIZATION OF PAYMENT OF ADJUSTED INTEREST ON THE AAMODT 
              SETTLEMENT PUEBLOS' FUND.

    Section 617(c)(1) of the Claims Resolution Act (Public Law 111-291) 
is amended by adding at the end the following:
                    ``(C) Adjusted interest payments.--In addition to 
                amounts made available subparagraphs (A) and (B), there 
                is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary 
                $4,314,709.18 to further assist the Pueblos in 
                accordance with subparagraph (B).''.
