                                 A BILL


 
   To restrict the possession, use, and acquisition of firearms and 
              ammunition by the Internal Revenue Service.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. RESTRICTION ON POSSESSION, USE, AND ACQUISITION OF FIREARMS 
              AND AMMUNITION BY THE INTERNAL REVENUE SERVICE.

    (a) Restriction on Possession and Use of Firearms.--Any officer or 
employee of the Internal Revenue Service hired after the date of the 
enactment of this Act--
            (1) shall not be in possession of a firearm while 
        performing official duties, and
            (2) shall not receive training in use of a firearm in 
        connection with such duties.
    (b) Prohibition on Acquisition of Firearms and Ammunition.--The 
Internal Revenue Service shall not, after the date of the enactment of 
this Act, acquire any firearm or ammunition.
