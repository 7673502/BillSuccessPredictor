                                 A BILL


 
   To repeal a restriction on the use of funds by the Securities and 
    Exchange Commission to ensure shareholders of corporations have 
               knowledge of corporate political activity.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL.

    Section 631 of the Financial Services and General Government 
Appropriations Act, 2021 (division E of Public Law 116-260) is hereby 
repealed.
