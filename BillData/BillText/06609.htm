                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit the Secretary of the Treasury from using facial recognition 
   technology to access any Internal Revenue Service online account.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Facial Authorization Cannot be 
Enforced Act'' or the ``FACE Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON FACIAL RECOGNITION FOR ACCESS TO INTERNAL 
              REVENUE SERVICE ACCOUNTS.

    The Secretary of the Treasury may not establish or maintain any 
verification process for access to an Internal Revenue Service online 
account using facial recognition technology.
