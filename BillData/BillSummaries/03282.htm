Drinking Water Funding for the Future Act of 2021

This bill reauthorizes through FY2026 several drinking water programs, such as programs concerning efforts to

  • assess risks to community water systems,
  • deploy innovative water technologies,
  • enforce compliance with standards under the Safe Drinking Water Act,
  • monitor unregulated drinking water contaminants,
  • assist water systems through the drinking water state revolving fund,
  • protect source waters,
  • test water quality in small and disadvantaged communities, and
  • reduce lead in drinking water.
