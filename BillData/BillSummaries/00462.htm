This bill directs the U.S. Postal Service, by 270 days after its enactment, to designate a single, unique ZIP code for each of

  • Miami Lakes, Florida;
  • Flanders, Northampton, and Riverside in the Town of Southampton, New York;
  • Ocoee, Florida;
  • Oakland, Florida;
  • Glendale, New York; and
  • Estero, Florida.
