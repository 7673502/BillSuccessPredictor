Laughlin/Bullhead International Airport Land Conveyance Act

This bill directs the Department of the Interior, at the request of Mohave County in Arizona, to convey approximately 10 acres of identified federal land (including any improvements) administered by the Bureau of Land Management to the county.

The federal land conveyed shall be used only for public purposes by the Mohave County Airport Authority.

Interior must convey the land without consideration and by quitclaim deed within one year of receiving the request from the county.
