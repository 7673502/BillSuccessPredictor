This bill makes technical amendments to the U.S. Code to revise references to statutory provisions in the following titles:

  • Title 5 (Government Organization and Employees),
  • Title 7 (Agriculture),
  • Title 11 (Bankruptcy),
  • Title 16 (Conservation),
  • Title 20 (Education),
  • Title 21 (Food and Drugs),
  • Title 26 (Internal Revenue Code),
  • Title 42 (The Public Health and Welfare),
  • Title 43 (Public Lands), and
  • Title 48 (Territories and Insular Possessions).
