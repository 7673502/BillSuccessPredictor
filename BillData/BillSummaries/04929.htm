Restore Everyone's Sleep Tonight Act of 2021 or the REST Act of 2021

This bill authorizes an airport to impose an access restriction between the hours of 10 p.m. and 7 a.m. for aircrafts.

An airport may impose a noise deterrence penalty on an air carrier or aircraft operator for a violation of the access restriction.

The restriction does not apply to the operation of military, law enforcement, and U.S. Coast Guard flights.
