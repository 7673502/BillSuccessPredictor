National Multimodal Freight Network Improvement Act

This bill revises the network components of the National Multimodal Freight Network (NMFN).

Specifically, the bill requires

  • the interim NMFN to include public ports of the United States that have a total annual cargo value of at least $1 billion, and
  • the final NMFN to include all specified interim network components when the Department of Transportation designates or redesignates the network.
