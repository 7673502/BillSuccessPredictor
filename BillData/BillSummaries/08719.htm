Great Bend of the Gila Conservation Act

This bill establishes the Great Bend of the Gila National Conservation Area and the Palo Verde National Conservation Area in Arizona.

The bill designates in Maricopa County in Arizona

  • approximately 9,809 acres as the Gila Bend Wilderness,
  • approximately 23,464 acres as the Red Rock Canyon Wilderness, and
  • approximately 23,712 acres as the Ringtail Wilderness.
