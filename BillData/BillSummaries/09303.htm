Saving Us from Pandemic Era Resistance by Building a Unified Global Strategy Act of 2022 or the SUPER BUGS Act of 2022

This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) to seek to enter into arrangements with foreign countries, multilateral organizations, and other appropriate entities to facilitate the development and commercialization of qualified pandemic or epidemic products, which include drugs, biological products, and medical devices. HHS shall report to Congress a strategy for achieving this goal.
